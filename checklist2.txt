Tourist Visa Document Checklist for Indian Citizens

COUNTRY: UAE (Dubai)
• Valid Passport (minimum 6 months validity, at least 2 blank pages)
• Scanned Copy of Passport (First and Last Page)
• Recent Passport Size Photograph (white background, taken within 3 months)
• Scanned Copy of PAN Card
• Pages of Passport Showing Previous International Travel
• Return/Onward Flight Tickets
• Confirmed Hotel Booking / Accommodation Proof
• Bank Statement (Last 3 Months)
